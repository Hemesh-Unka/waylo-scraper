After a hard day at work, or an afternoon in the sun, nothing beats a cold glass of beer. Which is why Countdown’s liquor range includes beers of every type. Whether you want an ale or a lager, something dry or something sweet, you’ll have plenty to choose from. And if you just googled “cider nz” you’re in luck too – our excellent cider collection includes traditional apple cider but also such delicious varieties as raspberry, and pear and ginger.

We’ve been sure to include favourite New Zealand beer brands such as Steinlager and Speights in our selection, as well great tasting beers produced by breweries such as Corona, Asahi and Peroni. And you’ll find convenient multipacks available in cans or bottles, whatever suits your preference – perfect for adding in to your online shopping order today.
